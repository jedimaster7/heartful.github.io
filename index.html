Heartful was created as a community involvement project, in this case to the global community, as a way to teach design principles, and eventually other subjects, in a beautiful way. The idea was conceived as I struggled to find free design lessons online, so I decided to take the information I found and turn it into a beautifully designed course that will always be free.

If you would like to help with this project, please visit our Github page. We'd love to have any help we can get, whether it is in creating lessons, fixing and developing the site, we definitely need lots of help.

Thanks, and I hope you enjoy this website! :)
